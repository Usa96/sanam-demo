Our Approach to ESG Risk Management

Our Policy & Framework

Taskforce

The ESG Risk Management Taskforce at Sanam Real Estate is comprised of a cross-sectional team across various disciplines and business units. The taskforce is led by senior management and is responsible for implementing and monitoring ESG Risk Management processes across the firm and at the investment level.

Framework

Sanam Real Estate's ESG Risk Management framework promotes sound environmental and social practices, encourages transparency and accountability, and supports positive development impacts throughout our business and investments.

Transparency

At Sanam Real Estate, we are committed to accountability and transparency in our ESG Risk Management initiatives. We report key sustainability metrics, benchmark the performance of our portfolio, and adhere to the highest industry standards to ensure responsible action planning with measurable outcomes.
