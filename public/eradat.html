Eradat International Real Estate Company

ERADAT Real Estate Investment Company, established in 2006 and restructured in 2010 as a Kuwait Shareholding Real Estate Company, is a leading Sharia-compliant investment firm. With a paid-up capital of KD 13.2 million and total assets valued at KD 54.4 million, ERADAT focuses on acquiring high-quality, sustainable, and diversified income-generating properties within Kuwait and the GCC.
