MEDCAP Medical and King’s College Hospital Dubai Form a Clinical Collaboration to Transform Healthcare in Kuwait

MEDCAP Medical and King’s College Hospital Dubai Form a Clinical Collaboration to Transform Healthcare in Kuwait

In a pioneering move to enhance healthcare standards in Kuwait, King’s College Hospital Dubai, and MEDCAP Medical, a subsidiary of Sanam Real Estate, have signed a clinical collaboration. This alliance aims to improve medical services, drive innovation, and introduce world-class healthcare solutions to the region with MEDCAP’s new medical center.

Aligning with Kuwait Vision 2035, this partnership seeks to expand access to leading medical expertise, as well as integrating the latest healthcare technologies, and empowering local medical professionals.

Advancing Healthcare in Kuwait

MEDCAP Medical, with its deep understanding of local healthcare needs and its strong commitment to patient care, is collaborating with leading international healthcare experts on a new medical center that introduces a visiting program with state-of-the-art medical care at its facility in Kuwait. The new MEDCAP medical center will integrate the latest medical technologies and specialized services, with a strong focus on patient experience and clinical excellence.

King's College Hospital Dubai continues its commitment to providing the Best of British Healthcare through this strategic partnership, focused on delivering quality care to the people of Kuwait. Under King’s comprehensive medical tourism services, this partnership offers much more than basic consultations, the robust system includes Multidisciplinary Team (MDT) meetings, acceptance of international referrals, and thorough follow-up care. This integrated approach ensures patients experience seamless, high-quality care throughout their medical journey between King's College Hospital Dubai and the new MEDCAP medical center, demonstrating a shared dedication to healthcare excellence for the Kuwait community.

Leaders Speak: A Shared Commitment to Excellence

"We are pleased to collaborate with MEDCAP Medical, as we believe that this partnership will contribute to enhancing the quality of healthcare and fostering innovation in medical services in Kuwait. We are committed to supporting advanced healthcare backed by global medical expertise, allowing patients access to the latest treatments and medical technologies. Our approach is designed for our experts to visit the new MEDCAP Medical center and collaborate with local doctors. Additionally, it allows local doctors the option to refer patients to us for a second opinion, thereby enhancing the quality of medical advice available.” Said Kimberley Pierce, CEO, King’s College Hospital Dubai

Dr. Sulaiman Al-Abduljader, Chairman of MEDCAP Medical, also emphasized the significance of this strategic collaboration, stating:

"We are proud of this strategic alliance, which will enable us to deliver cutting-edge medical solutions that improve quality of life and enhance healthcare services in the region."

Dr. Al-Abduljader further highlighted that the new medical center will include a comprehensive rehabilitation center equipped with the latest medical and rehabilitative devices to ensure optimal recovery and performance, whether in preventive treatment or post-surgical rehabilitation. The facility will also feature an advanced radiology unit and a fully integrated medical laboratory, allowing patients to access specialized and comprehensive services that meet the highest international standards.

A Hub for Innovation in Rehabilitation and Diagnostics

The facility will include a state-of-the-art rehabilitation center featuring the latest therapeutic and post-surgical recovery technologies to ensure the best patient outcomes. Additionally, it will house a world-class radiology unit providing precise diagnostic imaging to support accurate medical assessments and treatment plans. The medical center will also feature an integrated laboratory, offering advanced testing capabilities that enhance early detection and personalized care.

The rehabilitation services will be designed to support patients throughout their recovery journey, combining expert medical supervision with the latest rehabilitation techniques to promote faster healing and improved quality of life.

King’s College Hospital Dubai: Advancing Medical Education and Knowledge Exchange

As part of a broader commitment to medical excellence and professional development, a series of specialized medical symposiums will be launched in Kuwait to facilitate knowledge exchange, skill enhancement, and professional networking. These events will bring together leading global experts and local medical professionals to discuss key medical advancements, innovative treatment approaches, and the latest research, supporting the continuous development of healthcare providers in Kuwait.

Legal Disclaimer: The new medical facility in Kuwait is MEDCAP Medical Center and it is not affiliated with or branded as King’s College Hospital London or Dubai. King’s College Hospital Dubai’s involvement is limited to clinical advice and medical collaboration only, with no direct affiliation.

SANAM Real Estate Announces Acquisition and Transformation Plan

SANAM Real Estate Company’s Board of Directors convened on Monday, March 10, 2025, at 1:30 PM to discuss strategic decisions regarding the company’s growth and expansion.

As part of its investment strategy, SANAM Real Estate has approved the acquisition of a 30.285% stake in an educational company for a total value of USD 6,075,000 (equivalent to KWD 1,873,200). This transaction involves a related party and aligns with SANAM’s vision for long-term investments in high-growth sectors.

Additionally, the Board approved a major transformation plan and will submit the following recommendations to the General Assembly for approval:

  • Transitioning SANAM Real Estate from a real estate-focused company to a holding company.
  • Amending the company’s Articles of Association and Memorandum of Association to align its business operations with Sharia-compliant principles.
  • Approving a 100% capital increase at par value (100 fils per share), with no issuance premium.

The capital increase will raise SANAM’s authorized, issued, and paid-up capital from KWD 10,500,000 to KWD 21,000,000, subject to the approval of relevant regulatory authorities. All shares issued will be in cash, reflecting SANAM’s commitment to financial growth and investor confidence.

These decisions mark a significant milestone in SANAM Real Estate’s evolution and strategic repositioning, paving the way for diversified investment opportunities and enhanced shareholder value.

Arzan Financial Group and Sanam Real Estate Form Alliance to Acquire National Medical Company

Arzan Financial Group for Financing and Investment and Sanam Real Estate have announced a joint investment alliance to acquire 75% of the shares of the National Medical Industries Company (NATMED), a leading Kuwaiti company in the market of consumer products, food services, and medical consumables.

Commenting on this transaction, Jassem Hassan Zainal, Vice Chairman and CEO of Arzan Financial Group, stated: "This alliance reflects our strategy to invest in companies with competitive advantages and sustainable profitability. The National Medical Company was selected based on its solid position in the local market and its provision of products and services that meet the needs of various sectors. This acquisition represents a new step in the group's efforts to enhance its investment portfolio through high-quality, low-risk opportunities."

In the same context, Dr. Sulaiman Tariq Al-Abduljader, Vice Chairman and CEO of Sanam Real Estate, said: "We are delighted to collaborate with Arzan Financial Group in this strategic alliance, which aligns with our plans to expand and diversify income sources to enhance the company's performance and achieve our sustainability goals. This transaction will contribute to reinforcing Sanam's investment strategy, which is based on diversification and achieving stable returns. We are currently working on completing the acquisition procedures with the Competition Protection Authority and other official entities, and updates will be announced in due course."

NATMED was established in 1989 and is considered one of Kuwait's leading companies in the fields of consumer products, food services, and medical consumables. Its annual turnover exceeds 13 million Kuwaiti Dinars, with a focus on packaging, storage, and manufacturing activities for medical and food materials. The company boasts a broad range of more than 2,000 products and is the primary supplier to hotels, medical centers, restaurants, and supermarkets in Kuwait.

Sanam Establishes MEDCAP Medical and Forms Alliance with Dr. Muthanna Sartawi

Dr. Sulaiman Al Abduljader, Vice Chairman and CEO of SANAM Real Estate, announced the signing of a strategic alliance with Dr. Muthanna Sartawi to establish an integrated medical center. The announcement was made during the founding assembly meeting of MEDCAP Medical, a subsidiary of SANAM. Dr. Al Abduljader praised Dr. Sartawi's expertise and competence, as well as his regional and international reputation in orthopedic surgery and his development of advanced new systems in this field.

Dr. Al Abduljader also highlighted the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding with a global medical care company. The strategic alliance with Dr. Muthanna Sartawi and the healthcare company aligns with Kuwait’s Vision 2035, which aims to enhance and improve the quality of healthcare services provided in accordance with the highest global standards.

This initiative also seeks to develop national capabilities by targeting and empowering the best medical professionals while benefiting from the global expertise the medical center will offer, thus contributing to sustainable health development.

In this regard, Dr. Muthanna Sartawi appreciated the strategic partnership with SANAM Real Estate, aimed at achieving high-quality healthcare managed efficiently under the latest global healthcare systems. He noted that the medical center is in the process of being equipped and obtaining licenses from the relevant authorities. The center will include a world-class rehabilitation center specializing in physical therapy, equipped with the latest medical and rehabilitative devices, and highly trained staff to achieve optimal performance in both proactive physical therapy and post-surgical rehabilitation.

The medical center will also feature a radiology center and a laboratory to provide comprehensive services to patients and visitors, all with the main goal of offering world-class services.

About SANAM Real Estate

SANAM Real Estate, headquartered in Kuwait, is a publicly listed company founded in 1982 and listed on the Kuwait Stock Exchange in 2004 with a paid-up capital of 10.5 million Kuwaiti Dinars. The company specializes in long-term and strategic real estate investments in emerging markets. SANAM focuses on investing in high-quality real estate and innovative companies and projects, in partnership with specialized management teams and international partners, to achieve investment returns aligned with the region's sustainable development goals.

About Dr. Muthanna Sartawi

Dr. Muthanna Sartawi is a consultant orthopedic surgeon and Director of the Rapid Recovery Center for Joint Replacement at King's College Hospital in Dubai. Dr. Sartawi holds several patents registered with the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office in the field of orthopedic surgery. He is known for developing an innovative technique and specialized program for knee replacement surgeries using minimally invasive methods, allowing surgeries to be performed in a single day and significantly accelerating patient recovery.

Dr. Sartawi has performed over 3,000 successful surgeries with a success rate of 98%. His innovative techniques have earned him significant recognition, including awards from the Emir of Kuwait, the Crown Prince, the Minister of Health, and the Dubai Health Authority. Dr. Sartawi holds both American and Canadian board certifications in orthopedic surgery and is a specialist fellow at Rush Hospital in Chicago, one of the top orthopedic hospitals in the U.S.
