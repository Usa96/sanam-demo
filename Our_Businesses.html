Technical Integrated Aquaculture

Growth Capital

Technical Integrated Aquaculture

Technical Integrated Aquaculture (TIAC) is a forward-thinking company based in Kuwait, specializing in sustainable fish farming. Founded in 2020, TIAC operates a state-of-the-art aquaculture facility with a production capacity of 120-150 tons per year, utilizing Recirculatory Aquaculture System (RAS) technology. This modern method allows the company to raise fish in an eco-friendly, controlled environment, minimizing water usage and reducing environmental impact.

MEDCAP Medical Center

Growth Capital

MEDCAP Medical Center

MEDCAP Medical Clinic is a premier healthcare provider, specializing in comprehensive and patient-centered care. With a dedicated team of experts, MEDCAP offers a range of medical services including physiotherapy, podiatry, and foot surgery, ensuring optimal treatment for musculoskeletal conditions, sports injuries, and foot-related issues. The clinic's physiotherapy and podiatry services provide holistic care, focusing on rehabilitation, pain management, and preventive treatment for foot and lower limb disorders. MEDCAP also collaborates with renowned specialists from King's College Hospital Dubai, who visit the clinic regularly, offering patients access to top-tier medical expertise in areas such as advanced foot surgery. This partnership ensures that MEDCAP patients receive world-class care, right in their local community, without the need to travel abroad for complex treatments.

Abyan Building Construction Company

Private Equity

Abyan Building Construction Company

Abyan Building Construction is a pioneering construction company in Kuwait, revolutionizing the industry with advanced 3D concrete printing technology. Established to enhance construction efficiency and sustainability, Abyan has successfully completed landmark projects such as Kuwait's first 3D-printed residential villa and the world’s first large-scale 3D-printed water tanks. These innovations demonstrate significant cost savings and reduced environmental impact, making Abyan a leader in green construction solutions. By utilizing cutting-edge technology like the COBOD BOD2 printer, Abyan delivers faster, cost-effective, and more sustainable building solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Their commitment to innovation and quality is reflected in their mission to transform traditional construction methods and offer smart, eco-friendly alternatives for the future​
